Release 26.0.317.0 (PR #463) - 01/10/2025

✨ Features

💰 Finance

  • Added Refresh Packaging Taxes action to multiple invoice and credit memo pages, allowing users to easily update tax information across documents. [albertodone]
  • Updated due date calculation to include non-working days, ensuring more accurate payment schedules. [mpisana]

🛍️ Sales

  • Added Your Reference field to Blanket Purchase Orders and Blanket Sales Orders, enabling users to track specific references for better order management. [mpisana]

🪲 Bug Fixes

💰 Finance

  • Renamed field Amount Including VAT to Amt. Including VAT, improving clarity in financial reports. [albertodone]
  • Corrected packaging tax entries calculation to manage Tax Rates date validity, ensuring accurate tax reporting. [albertodone]

📦 Logistic

  • Added drill-down functionality for Sales Orders and Transfer Orders in Warehouse Shipment, enhancing visibility and tracking of shipments. [mpisana]

🔧 Other Changes

⚙️ System

  • Renamed field to Ship-to Country Code, providing clearer information for shipping processes. [albertodone]
