Release Notes & Version History

Release 26.0.317.0 (PR #463) - 01/10/2025

✨ Features

💰 Finance

  • Added Refresh Packaging Taxes action to multiple invoice and credit memo pages, allowing users to easily update tax information across several documents at once. [albertodone]
  • Updated due date calculation to include non-working days, ensuring that users have more accurate timelines for payment and delivery. [mpisana]

🛍️ Sales

  • Added Your Reference field to Blanket Purchase Orders and Blanket Sales Orders, enabling users to include specific references for better tracking and communication. [mpisana]

🪲 Bug Fixes

💰 Finance

  • Renamed field Amount Including VAT to IPL Amt. Including VAT for clarity in financial reporting. [albertodone]
  • Corrected packaging tax entries calculation to manage Tax Rates date validity, ensuring accurate tax calculations for invoices. [albertodone]

📦 Logistic

  • Added drill-down functionality for Sales Orders and Transfer Orders in Warehouse Shipment, allowing users to easily access detailed information on orders. [mpisana]

🔧 Other Changes

⚙️ System

  • Renamed field to Ship-to Country Code, improving clarity in shipping documentation. [albertodone]
  • Other: Added missing semicolon in parameter declaration for CreatePackagingTaxEntries procedure, enhancing system stability. [albertodone]
